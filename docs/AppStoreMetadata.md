## Promotional text

Simple posture reminders that stay out of your way and show up on the schedule you choose.

## Description

Posture Check is a lightweight menu bar app for macOS that reminds you to sit tall throughout the day.

Set a reminder interval, limit reminders to working hours, and keep them to weekdays if you want. Posture Check sends local notifications only, stores settings on your Mac, and stays focused on one job.

### Features

- Menu bar app with a minimal settings window
- Local reminders on a repeating interval
- Optional working-hours schedule
- Weekdays-only reminders
- Launch at login
- No analytics, accounts, or cloud sync
